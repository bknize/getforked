date: 2021-09-07
tags: [dessert, knize]
---

Bake at 450 for 10min
Then 375 for 40-50min

## Filling

* 1/3 cup packed brown sugar
* 3/4 cup sugar
* 1/2 tsp cinnamon
* 1/4 tsp salt
* 1 tsp grated lemon rind or juice
* 2 Tbsp flour
* 2 Tbsp butter
* 6 cups apple slices

Combine in bowl; cover apples pot [ sic ] butter.

## Crust

* 2 cups flour
* 1 tsp salt
* 2/3 cup crisco
* 5 - 6 tsp cold water
